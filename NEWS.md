# dietrecallkit 0.1.0

* Initial CRAN submission.
* Added functions for harmonizing dietary recall food and ingredient data.
* Added functions for identifying non-gram foods and computing actual gram intake.
* Added support for preserving `food_details_rowid` in detailed gram intake outputs.
* Added functions for computing dietary outcomes and indicators, including Minimum Dietary Diversity, Global Dietary Recommendations, Global Diet Quality Score, actual nutrient intake, usual nutrient intake, EAR adequacy, probability of nutrient inadequacy, Nutrient Adequacy Ratio, Household Food Insecurity Access Scale, and dietary species richness.
